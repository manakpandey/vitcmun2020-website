Letter from the Secretary-General


It is my absolute privilege to announce the Sixth edition of the VIT Chennai Model United Nations.

Being exposed to the concept of Model United Nations as a concept pretty early into my school life, I had the opportunity of understanding all about these events. I was introduced to Model United Nations conferences as a public speaking competition. I was told that these events can serve as a brilliant stepping stone to help me understand how to improve my oratory and debating skills. Even though this was not the full picture, it did provide a good insight into the same.

As I participated in more MUNs along the way I understood that the purpose of a Model UN Conference is not just to help one improve their public speaking abilities. It’s to generate and spark interest in the work that the United Nations engages in. It is to raise awareness about the many critical issues that are many times overlooked by an average citizen. Model UN conferences help in incredibly improving critical thinking, reasoning, and logical analysis. It gives participants a chance to understand the insights behind raw data and figures. With the Internet at our disposal nowadays, it is incredibly easy to get information on world issues. Statistics are readily available. MUNs empower individuals to take insights from the competitive debate and look at the bigger picture.

VIT Chennai Model United Nations has consistently been one of the best conferences in India. We have always hosted brilliant competition across multiple types of committees in our previous editions. MUNs are brilliant forums for knowledge and understanding issues of International importance, be it from the Geopolitical, Economic, Social, Security or Cultural arena. I firmly believe VITCMUN stands for the academic nature of these conferences.

VIT Chennai Model United Nations has always been envisioned as a conference that fosters competition to its maximum potential. Our Executive Board members are always selected through an elaborate selection process. With an extensive understanding of International affairs and brilliant experience, they have time and again ensured that the delegates take insightful learning back home after the conference.

VIT Chennai Model UN has been providing breakthroughs in delegate resources time and again. Our Tech team, throughout the last three editions, brought immense progress in the form of E-Chits to the Chennai MUN Circuit. Developed completely by a team of highly motivated students, it does provide an alternative approach to the question of chits in a Model UN Conference. Our E-Chit system facilitated a grand total of 3129 chits in VITCMUN 2019. This time, we will be trying to expand on this concept and try to ensure ease of access for council proceedings in more areas.

It is with great pride and honor, that I invite you to the Sixth Edition of this conference on the 20th, 21st and 22nd of March, 2020.

Siddharth Banerjee
Secretary-General,
VITCMUN 2020.
